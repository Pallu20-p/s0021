HISTORY

KLE Society, founded in 1916, has been playing a key role in the educational and socio-economic uplift of North Karnataka region. Seven Saintly Teachers, named â€œSAPTARSHIS", a group of seven self-sacrificing, devoted and inspirational juvenile graduates had determined to institute an educational organization. They had placed before them the ideals of Truth, Love, Service and Self-Sacrifice as beacon lights. With poignant desires, abundant imagination and a mission to fulfill, the founder life-members started Anglo Vernacular School on 13th November, 1916 in the fort vicinity of Belagavi, which heralded the beginning of the Karnatak Lingayat Education Society. Over the years, a variety of institutions from Nursery School to Post-Graduation found their place in the educational map of the KLE Society. At present, the Society is running the institutions in all the spheres of knowledge such as Humanities, Physical Science, Medical Science, Agricultural Science, Engineering and Technology, etc. The society has entered into collaboration with as many as fourteen foreign Universities.
